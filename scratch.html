Travel has shaped who I am. Living abroad, experiencing new cultures, and stepping outside my comfort zone has given me perspective that extends far beyond any classroom.

  • Europe — Exploring the history, architecture, and diverse cultures across the continent. Each country offers unique lessons and perspectives.
